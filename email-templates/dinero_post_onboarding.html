Which invoices can I get paid out early?

There are some criteria for an invoice to be eligible for early payment. Here are the main ones:

Business to business

Invoices sent to registered businesses in Denmark.

Invoice amount

If the total invoice amount is minimum 5.000kr

Payment term

Invoices with more than 7 day payment terms

Customers with good history

Not all of your customers may have enough history for us to offer early payment

*Other factors may deem service unavailable

Frequently asked questions

How do I sell my first invoice?

Create your invoice as usual and save it as a draft. Then go to flow.moneyflow.io/dinero/login and continue the flow from there.

When do I receive the money?

You will receive the money within 2 hours (weekdays, between 8am and 6pm)

How will this affect my customer?

Your customer receives and pays the invoice as usual, but uses the new bank information stated on the invoice
